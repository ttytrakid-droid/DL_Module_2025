Module Syllabus

During the course we will cover the following topics:

  • Introduction to Deep Learning
  • Basic DL concepts (backprop, regularisation, etc).
  • PyTorch primer
  • Feed-forward Networks (FFNs)
  • Convolutional neural networks (CNNs)
  • Probability for DL
  • Generative models: Variational autoencoders (VAEs)
  • Generative adversarial networks (GANs)
  • Diffusion models
  • Recurrent neural networks (RNNs) and long short-term memory networks (LSTMs)
  • Transformers
  • Other DL methods, architectures, and strategies (informative session, not assessed)
